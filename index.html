FAQ

Who can I contact if I want to speak directly with someone to collaborate or get info?

Contact the team with this mail contact@bimble.cc, we’ll get back in touch asap!

How can I upload my pictures on the site?

You need to contact us via email and we’ll get them online.

What type of artists can join Bimble?

Any sort of visual art is welcomed in this platform like paintings, photographs, graffiti, prints and we are open to creative alternative expressions of art.

What sort of business can join?

Bars, hotels, coffee shops, clothing stores, travel agencies, furniture, decoration shops, galleries and others.

How do I get to collaborate as an artists with a business space?

Once you have your art in our site, we will find a space for you asap and we’ll contact you with the great news!

Who would buy the art on the walls?

Clients entering in the business via online payment.

How would the artist receive payment?

The artist receives the payment online.

How could the artist promote collaborations with the business?

Artists and business could agree to organize events in the space, this will help both the artists gaining visibility and the business.

Why should I trust leaving my art in a shop?

We will have a review system, selecting the business and artists that take part in the community.
